Image super-resolution

The project aims to develop a model which generates high definition images from the given image. The objective is to enhance the resolution and quality of low resolution image to generate attractive images with high resolution. The model is trained on a dataset of paired low and high resolution images.

Volcano Eruption analysis

The ultimate goal of the project is to provide a useful tool for predicting volcanic activity and providing early warning to communities that may be affected. This has the potential to make a significant contribution to the field of volcano eruption prediction and could have important practical applications in helping to protect communities and prevent loss of life and property.

Public Grievance Redressal App

The Public Grievance Redressal Portal is an online platform designed to facilitate the submission, processing, and resolution of public complaints. The portal provides a user-friendly interface for individuals to register, login, and report grievances they encounter in their daily lives. The system incorporates three key roles: users, Grievance Routing Officers (ROs), and Grievance Redressal Officers (GROs), each with distinct responsibilities and access levels.
