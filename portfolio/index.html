Who I am

Hello there! I'm Mayura from Pelthaje.

Curiosity about the world often ignites a journey across a multitude of diverse environments and cultures. This sense of wonder frequently leads to exploring varied landscapes, from verdant jungles to lively urban streets. The essence of exploration extends beyond merely visiting new locations; it involves immersing oneself in different perspectives, engaging meaningfully with individuals from various backgrounds, and uncovering the unique narratives that each place has to offer. Such experiences not only broaden one’s understanding but also bring a sense of joy and fulfilment in discovering the true character of each destination.

Guided by principles of curiosity, openness, and empathy, these explorations are driven by a desire to connect with the world in a deeper way. The prospect of future adventures generates excitement and the chance to share these valuable experiences with others. Thank you for being a part of this journey. Should you wish to discuss topics related to travel, photography, science, swimming, driving, or general exploration, please feel free to get in touch.

    The anticipation of the monsoon is a blend of longing and excitement. As the earth thirsts for the first drops, the sky darkens, signalling the arrival of the season of renewal. The scent of petrichor—the fresh aroma of rain hitting dry soil—fills the air, awakening memories and emotions. The symphony of raindrops, the dance of the wind, and the vibrant transformation of the landscape create a magical experience. The monsoon is a time of reflection, growth, and rejuvenation, where nature’s rhythm brings a sense of peace and harmony to our lives.
