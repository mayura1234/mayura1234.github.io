Who I am

Hi there! I'm Mayura from Pelthaje, a passionate explorer, avid learner, and lover of all things adventurous. Growing up in Kasaragod, I was always curious about the world beyond my backyard. This curiosity led me on a journey across states, where I’ve hiked through lush jungles, dived into vibrant ponds, and wandered through bustling city streets. My love for exploration isn't just about seeing new places—it's about immersing myself in different cultures, connecting with people, and discovering the stories that make each place unique.

I'm capturing the perfect sunset on camera, writing about a heartwarming encounter with a local, or analyzing data to uncover hidden patterns, I find joy in every aspect of my journey. Photography is one of my greatest passions. Through the lens, I aim to capture the beauty and essence of the places I visit and the people I meet. Each photograph tells a story, preserving a moment in time that speaks to the heart of exploration. My core values of curiosity, openness, and empathy guide me in everything I do. Looking ahead, I'm excited to explore new destinations, embark on more adventures, and continue sharing my journey with you. Thank you for joining me on this incredible adventure! Feel free to reach out if you’d like to connect, collaborate, or simply chat about travel, photography, science, swimming, driving, and exploration.
